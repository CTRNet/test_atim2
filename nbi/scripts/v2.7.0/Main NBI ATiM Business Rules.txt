
I - Clinical Annotation

The list of data that could be linked to a participant is limited to:
   - Participant message.
   - Participant clinical annotation with only one 'lab' record type named 'Retrospective Cancer Specimen Annotation - Breast'.
   - Participant Collection.
   
The participant to collection(s) link can only be completed by the defintion of a clinical annotation linked to this collection event. 
No consent and/or treatment definition can complet the participant to collection(s) link.

II - Collection

A collection can not be created into the system but not linked to a participant. Participant of a collection is a required information.

Collection content (sample and aliquot types) is limited to tissue.

Aliquot barcodes are generated by the system.

Aliquot labels are required and should be unique.
